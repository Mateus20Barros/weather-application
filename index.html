Weather Application

Hey there ...

Weather Application is a system that works with weather conditions in several cities around the world using the AccuWeather API to obtain weather information.

When performing a search, you will get weather information of the city such as: City name, time zone indicating whether it is Day or Night, Weather and Temperature.

This information will be shown on a revolving card just below the research button on the side.

As we use third-party APIs, you will only be able to perform 50 searches with the Weather Application.

Enjoy !
